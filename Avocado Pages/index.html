The Spring College is a unit under Christ Ambassador. Students' Outreach, Rivers State University Chapter. It is one of the units recognized as CASOR National Level. It goes by different names in different local chapters such as -Ambassadors Bible College, Foundation Bible Class, etc. no matter the name a chapter chooses to adopt, the aims and objectives remain the same. In RSU, the Spring College is provided for in Article 5, section 6 of CASOR Constitution.
