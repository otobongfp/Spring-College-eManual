About Us

The Spring College is a unit under Christ Ambassador. Students' Outreach, Rivers State University Chapter. It is one of the units recognized as CASOR National Level. It goes by different names in different local chapters such as -Ambassadors Bible College, Foundation Bible Class, etc. no matter the name a chapter chooses to adopt, the aims and objectives remain the same. In RSU, the Spring College is provided for in Article 5 of CASOR Constitution.
The idea behind the site is that any newcomer or springer in the fellowship can easily gain access to information about the fellowship and get started towards becoming a member. The site does not just end here but will be useful for the coming generation. Information about past offices will be added subsequently with access to the constitution.
