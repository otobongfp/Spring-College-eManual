This has to do with our peculiarity, uniqueness and difference from others. It is a scope of guide for the way we live and conduct our lives in this household of faith. In fact, this topic defines our personality and distinguishes us as Ambassadors on campus. The university is a community that brings together people from all backgrounds, the unsaved, the good, the bad, the corrupt, etc. as believers on campus, we ought to live as the salt of the earth. 5. Sharing Rooms with the Unsaved
When we live in the same room with unbelievers, we must live as light. We should not mastermind trouble making or quarreling. We must live honestly and as much as it lies in us, live at peace with all our roommates and neighbors. However, we must not allow ourselves to be harassed, intimidated or threatened by anyone. In any case of harassment or threat should be reported to the hostel hall pastor who shall bring such case to the president if need be. As believers, we ought to comply with the rules and regulations of our hostels or blocks, so long as they are not inconsistent with the provisions of the bible.
